Twisco Watch

Twisco Watch is a special watch which is able to tell time,read heartbeat, pulse and tempertature.It is also loaded with a fully customized state of the art A.I.(Artificial Intelligence)which helps you in your day to day activities.

Vision

Vision is a state of the art eyeglass which is able to connect to your brain nerves, and give you the ability to control linked devices with your mind wherever, whenever you are. It is loaded with a fully functional A.I.Which is able to communicate with you and you are able to watch videos,read papers,listen to music and so on with the 'VISION'.

A.T(Automated television)

A.T is a special television which is able to automatically run itself. It can On and Off itself, it knows when to stay on and when to shut down. It is also customized with a working A.I.(Artificial Intelligence)which helps your activities and allows it to control itself without stressing you.
